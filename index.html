Midnight Snack

I was hungry and I made some scrambled eggs

You Will Need:
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Tacobell Mild Sauce
Follow These Steps:

  1. Put pan on the stove and heat it up on low

  2. Put butter on the pan

  3. Crack egg into pan and mixed until cooked

  4. Plate Egg

  5. Put Taco bell mild sauce on top

  6. Enjoy
